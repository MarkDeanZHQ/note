# 07
拟声拟形类

- schedule    
- n.日程，行程，计划，安排     v.安排
- 拟声：s-该死    che-车    du-堵    该死,又堵车了.下次要安排好计划.
- The agency has arranged an absolutely exhausting schedule for the trip.
- 旅行社安排的旅行日程令人疲惫不堪

- function 
- n.功能；函数；职责    v.运转，起作用
- 拟声：防个身 是 防狼喷雾的 — 功能,起作用
- Her brain/liver is functioning normally.

- fuss
- n./v. 大惊小怪，小题大做
- 拟声：发丝-小事
- I don't know what the fuss is all about!
- 我不知道这有什么大惊小怪的！

- awe  [ɔː]  v.使敬畏    n.敬畏
- awesome  ['ɔːsəm]   adj.令人敬畏的；很好的
- 拟声：哇哦    见到万里长城,怀着敬畏的感叹
- 后缀-some  令人..样的
- The children stared at the teacher with awe.
- They had an awesome task ahead.

- candidate     ['kændɪdət]
- n.候选人，应聘者；应试者
- 拟声：can did ate   --   can did it  候选人是可以做事,有能力的人
- a candidate for the presidency / the job.
- 总统候选人/工作求职者

- dome    n.圆屋顶    -ome-表示空间的后缀   
- 拟形：d-D 逆时针旋转180度-圆屋顶
- domestic    adj.国内的;家内的    -est 最  -ic adj.   最圆屋顶的-国家
- dominate    v.控制,支配
- dominant    adj.占优势的,强大的
- 溯源记忆: 圆屋顶房屋在过去是西方皇室家族居住的
- The stronger man dominate the weaker.
- 强者支配弱者.
- Peace was the dominant theme of the conference.
- 和平是大会的首要议题.

- volume    n.容量，容积；音量
- The volume of the cup.
- vase     ase-后缀    v-容器   n.花瓶

- orient    n.东方     太阳(o)升起(ri)的地方(ent)
- r-小草向上生长  rise 升起 ri(词根上升) + ise(后缀)
- oriental    adj.东方的
- China is the biggest country in the orient.
- He specializes in oriental history.

- horizon    n.地平线;视野
- 拟声：ho-火   riz-ris(升起)     火红的太阳升起的地方
- horizontal    adj.水平的,地平的
- The sun rose above the horizon.
- The sun rises from the horizontal line.

- ambulance    n.救护车
- 拟声：俺不能死
- We will send the abmulance to pick him up.

- ambition [æm'bɪʃn]   n.野心
- 拟声：俺必胜
- ambitious    adj.有野心的
- His ambition has come true.
- Mr. Lin is an ambitious businessman.

- massage  [ mə'sɑːʒ ]  v./n.按摩
- 拟声：马杀鸡
- She is having a facial massage.

- laser    n.激光
- 拟声：镭射
- Lasers can be used to perform operations nowadays

- intelligent    adj.有智慧的，聪明的
- 拟声：intel-英特尔 — 世界五百强企业 很聪明的人在里面工作
- 词根词缀：intel-inter-在...中间  lig-挑选  人群中挑选出的 有智慧的
- intelligence    n.智慧
- diligent    adj.勤奋的
- 拟声：地里种的人 — 农民伯伯 — 勤奋的人
- diligence    n.勤奋
- It was an intelligent decision.
- He is the most diligent student in our class.

- dumb    adj.哑的；愚蠢的
- 拟声：大默    大爱无言，大默无声。
- She is very kind to the dumb children.

- obese    adj.肥胖的
- 拟声：我必死  —  肥胖的发病率高
- Obese patients are advised to change their diet.

- curl    n.卷发  v.弄卷
- 拟声：c-卷的字母   ur-元音+r 卷舌  l-卷舌音
- She doesn't curl her hair any longer.

- gloom    n.忧郁  v.便黑暗
- 拟形：gloo-9100   m-meter  跑九千一百米 使人抑郁,跑到两眼发黑
- gloomy    adj.忧郁的
- The news cast a deep gloop over the whole family.
- She is always in that gloomy mood.

- roar    v.咆哮
- 拟声：与猛兽低吼的声音相近
- The injured man roared in pain.

- radar [ 'reɪdɑr ]    n.雷达  拟声
- There are enemy aircraft on the radar screen.

- vain    adj.徒劳的，无用的
- 通假: ain-air    v-容器      装空气的容器 无用的
- vacant    adj.空着的
- 词根：vac-empty-空
- All my efforts were in vain.
- The lavatory is vacant.
- 厕所现在无人使用

- vacuum    n.真空
- 词根: vac-空     通假 uu-vv- 空  真的空
- The food is kept in the absolute vacuum.

- bind    v.捆绑
- bound    adj.绑缚的;约束的
- 拟声：绑的
- abound    v.大量存在的
- a-否定,加强   不约束的 —  泛滥、大量存在
- She was bound to a chair.
- Venice abounds in famous hotels.

- bound    adj.绑缚的；约束的
- boundary    n.边界   绑起来整个国家
- national boundaries    国界

- academy    n.学院，专科院校；研究院
- 拟声：啊，开导我(me)     
- The British Academy of Film and Television Arts.
- 英国电影和电视艺术学院

- yawn    v.打哈欠
- 拟声：打哈欠声
- He stood up, stretched and yawned.

- leap    v.跳跃
- 拟声：立扑    — 立刻扑上去
- Think twice before you leap.
- 三思而后行。

- dome    n.圆屋顶
- dorm    n.宿舍
